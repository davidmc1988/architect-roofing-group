Built On Heritage

Roofing isn't just our profession — it's our heritage.

Founded by Anthony New, Architect Roofing Group combines over a decade of hands-on roofing expertise with an uncompromising commitment to quality, professionalism and craftsmanship.

Coming from a family roofing background spanning more than 30 years, we specialise in both modern and traditional systems — delivering high-quality roofing, waterproofing and specialist detailing for residential, commercial, heritage and new-build projects across Merseyside, Cheshire, Greater Manchester, North Wales and throughout the UK.

From heritage slate and leadwork to advanced liquid waterproofing, we take pride in protecting, enhancing and future-proofing every building entrusted to us.

What We Do

Traditional craftsmanship, modern systems.

From heritage slate and hand-dressed leadwork to industry-leading liquid waterproofing and solar integration — every discipline delivered by the same expert hands.

01

Pitched Roofing

Natural & reclaimed Welsh slate, clay and concrete tiling, heritage restoration and full new-build packages — roof replacements and refurbishments built to last generations.

02

Specialist Leadwork

Lead aprons, box gutters, flashings, valley gutters and bespoke detailing — including heritage leadwork installations dressed entirely by hand.

03

Flat Roofing & Waterproofing

Certified Sika liquid systems, Restec, Scott Bader and AH-25 Hydrostop — balcony and podium-deck waterproofing through to large-scale commercial flat roofing.

04

Solar Roofing Integration

Solar-ready roof installations and heritage-sensitive detailing — full roof preparation and specialist weatherproofing around every solar array.

05

Project Management

NVQ Level 6 site management and SSSTS-qualified supervision, full health & safety compliance, quality-assurance inspections and complete project coordination.
